# UniGram Campus Launch Playbook
**Prepared by: Kofi Darko, Brand & Growth Strategist — Hashtechs**
**Pilot Campus: KNUST, Kumasi | Template for All Campus Launches**
**Date: May 2026**

## Overview

This playbook is the operating manual for launching UniGram at any university campus. Every step is documented, every resource is templated, every metric is defined. The goal: 500 verified student sign-ups within the first two weeks on campus — and a community that's already buzzing before the wider national launch. This document starts with KNUST (our pilot, our home base) and is designed to be replicated verbatim at every subsequent campus.

---

## Pilot Campus: KNUST — Why We Start Here

Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology, Kumasi. This is where our CEO, Hashtag, is based. KNUST has approximately 40,000 students, one of the most active student unions in Ghana (SRC KNUST), a vibrant campus culture, a strong engineering culture that will appreciate a tech product, and enough student WhatsApp groups to make any marketer's head spin. KNUST students are loud, proud, and community-driven. If UniGram works here, it works anywhere.

## Pre-Launch Phase (2 Weeks Before Launch Day)

### Week -2: Groundwork

**Day 1–2: Identify the Campus Ambassador**
The Campus Ambassador is the most critical hire in this entire playbook. They are not a paid staffer — they are a student who bleeds campus life. The ideal KNUST ambassador:
- Is in Level 200 or Level 300 (not a fresher, not about to graduate)
- Has 300–2,000 followers on Instagram or TikTok
- Is known on campus — course rep, hall leader, society officer, or just naturally popular
- Has a smartphone and is comfortable posting daily
- Is enrolled in any programme but ideally in Business, Communication, or Engineering
- Speaks both Twi and English fluently

**Recruitment method:** Post a call-out on existing KNUST WhatsApp groups via SRC contact. Hold a 30-minute virtual interview. Select one primary ambassador and one backup.

**Day 3–4: Onboarding the Ambassador**
- Set up their UniGram account with a verified Campus Ambassador badge
- Give them a full app walkthrough (1-hour video call or in-person with the Hashtechs team)
- Provide the Ambassador Kit (see Replication Kit section below)
- Add them to the Hashtechs Ambassador WhatsApp group
- Grant them GH₵ 50 mobile data immediately (builds trust and shows we are serious)

**Day 5–7: SRC Partnership Activation**
- Formal meeting with KNUST SRC President or Public Relations Officer
- Present UniGram's value to students (not just as an app — as a campus infrastructure tool)
- Secure commitment for: one Instagram post from the SRC's official account, a mention in the next SRC newsletter or broadcast, and a table at the next SRC-organized event
- Give the SRC President their own verified account with the "SRC Leader" badge

**Day 8–10: Campus Reconnaissance**
- Ambassador does a physical survey of campus hotspots: Unity Hall junction, KNUST Market area, Faculty of Engineering noticeboard, KNUST Library entrance, Student Representative Council office, main canteen areas
- Identify the top 5 poster locations (highest daily footfall)
- Identify the top 3 digital screens on campus (if any) for potential digital display partnership

**Day 11–14: Pre-Launch Buzz Building**
- Ambassador starts posting teaser content (templates in Replication Kit below): "Something is coming to KNUST. You've been waiting for this. Watch this space. 👀 #UniGram #KNUST"
- Ambassador posts 1x per day (Stories only) in the 5 days before launch
- Posters go up at all 5 identified locations — each with QR code
- WhatsApp broadcast blast goes out to SRC contact list (Day 13): "KNUST students — something big drops [launch date]. Set your reminders. It's for us, by us."
- Goal by end of pre-launch: 300+ people have seen the teaser content, 100+ have clicked through the waitlist link

## Launch Week (Days 1–7)

### The Target: 300 Sign-Ups in 7 Days

**Launch Day (Day 1): The Big Moment**

- Ambassador posts a proper launch announcement at 8:00 AM: a 30–60 second TikTok/Reel showing them opening UniGram for the first time, reacting genuinely, then dropping the download link. Caption template: *"KNUST finally has its own social network and I'm not playing. Download UniGram right now — I'll see you inside. Link in bio. #UniGram #KNUST #KNUSTStudents"*
- SRC official page reposts or shares at 10:00 AM
- WhatsApp broadcast blast: "UniGram is LIVE at KNUST. Download now, it's free. [link]"
- Deploy ambassador to 2 high-traffic locations with a branded phone stand to show students the app in person, assist with downloads, and take selfies

**Ambassador Posting Schedule (Launch Week)**

| Time | Platform | Content Type |
|---|---|---|
| 8:00 AM | Instagram/TikTok | Launch announcement (Reel/TikTok) |
| 12:30 PM | Instagram Stories | "I'm using UniGram right now — join me" live screenshot |
| 6:00 PM | X (Twitter) | Quote-tweet any campus-related trending topic + UniGram mention |

This 3x/day cadence continues for all 7 days of launch week. The ambassador is the face — let them be natural, not scripted.

**UniGram Launch Party (Day 3 or Day 4)**

The launch party is a physical activation. Keep it simple, energetic, and shareable.

- **Location:** KNUST — outdoors at Unity Hall area, near the Great Hall forecourt, or a central open space near KNUST SRC
- **Duration:** 2 hours (4 PM – 6 PM, after afternoon lectures)
- **Format:**
  - Small speaker setup (Bluetooth speaker + playlist — Amapiano + Afrobeats + Highlife)
  - Branded table with UniGram merchandise display
  - "Download UniGram and Win" station — students download the app, show the ambassador, get entered in a raffle
  - Live posting challenge: first 3 students to post something on UniGram and tag the event win a prize instantly
- **Giveaway prizes:**
  - 1st prize: MTN 10GB data bundle
  - 2nd prize: Infinix phone case or PopSocket (branded)
  - 3rd–10th prizes: UniGram branded sticker packs + 2GB data each
- **Documentation:** Ambassador films the entire event for content. Post same day.

**Estimated cost of launch party:** GH₵ 600–900 (data prizes + branded merchandise + minimal setup)

**Days 5–7: Momentum Maintenance**
- Ambassador DMs every new sign-up personally to welcome them (can use a template but personalize with their name)
- Post daily "UniGram of the Day" — best student post from campus that day, reshared with permission
- Run a 48-hour poll on X: "What feature should we build next for KNUST students?" — drives product feedback AND engagement

## Post-Launch Phase (Weeks 2–4)

### Week 2: Retention Drive

**Sign-up Audit:** Check actual verified KNUST sign-ups in the admin dashboard daily. If below 200 by Day 8, trigger a secondary giveaway blast.

- Secondary giveaway: "Sign up before [date], share your profile, and win MTN 5GB data." Simple, fast.
- Ambassador hosts a 20-minute Instagram Live on their personal account using UniGram — showing their feed, exploring the confession board, checking the campus marketplace
- Push one campus-specific piece of content: "The Top 5 Places to Eat at KNUST According to UniGram Users" — a real post using actual student submissions from the app. This drives the flywheel: app generates content, content drives downloads.

### Week 3: Community Building

- Encourage department societies to create official pages on UniGram (provide a guide)
- KNUST Engineering Society, KNUST Medical Students' Association, KNUST Business School Student Association — all get their own verified pages + a shout-out post
- Ambassador moderates the KNUST Confession Board for toxic content and screenshots the best (non-harmful) confessions for Instagram Stories
- Collect 10 written testimonials from early users for use in marketing materials

### Week 4: Feedback and Iteration

- Deploy an in-app survey: "What do you love? What's broken? What's missing?"
- Schedule a Zoom call with the top 10 most active users (by posts, by sign-ups referred) — these are your super users. Listen to them.
- Push any OTA (over-the-air) updates addressing critical bugs within 48 hours of identification
- Prepare a "Week 4 Campus Report" for the Hashtechs team: sign-ups, DAU, top content, bugs logged, NPS score

## Ambassador Incentives & Program Structure

Ambassadors are not free labour. They are the most important marketing asset we have.

| Benefit | Details |
|---|---|
| Exclusive Verified Badge | "Campus Ambassador" badge on their UniGram profile — visible to all users |
| Monthly Data Allowance | GH₵ 50 mobile data (any network) per month, paid on the 1st |
| Branded Merchandise | UniGram hoodie or t-shirt, sticker pack, phone case |
| Monthly Cash Bonus | GH₵ 100 bonus if the campus hits the 500 sign-up target |
| Referral Incentive | GH₵ 2 per verified sign-up referred beyond the 500 target (up to GH₵ 200/month) |
| Priority Beta Access | First to get new features; input on product roadmap |
| Certificate of Completion | Hashtechs official certificate after 3-month ambassador term |
| LinkedIn Recommendation | Personal LinkedIn recommendation from the CEO after successful term |

Ambassador term: 3 months. Renewable based on performance.

## Campus Target Metrics

| Metric | Target | Timeline |
|---|---|---|
| Total verified sign-ups | 500 | 2 weeks post-launch |
| Daily Active Users (DAU) | 150+ | By end of Week 3 |
| Posts created by campus users | 200+ | By end of Week 2 |
| Ambassador follower growth | +200 followers (personal) | Launch week |
| App Store reviews from campus | 10+ (4-star or above) | By end of Week 4 |
| WhatsApp broadcast list size | 300+ contacts | By launch day |
| Department pages created | 5+ | By end of Week 3 |

If the 500 sign-up target is not hit in 2 weeks, the campus is not considered "activated" and a recovery sprint is deployed (extended giveaway, ambassador reinforcement, paid WhatsApp blast).
