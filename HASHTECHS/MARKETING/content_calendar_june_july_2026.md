## Overview and Strategy

Eight weeks. Three platforms. One goal: make UniGram the most talked-about app on every Ghanaian campus before the third week of July. This calendar is not a list of posts — it is a campaign architecture. Every week has a narrative theme. Every post serves that theme. Every platform plays its role: Instagram builds brand equity, TikTok drives reach and downloads, X drives conversation and keeps us culturally relevant.

**Platform Roles:**
- **Instagram:** Polished, aspirational, community-celebrating. Students save our posts, share our Reels, see us as the premium version of campus culture.
- **TikTok:** Raw, funny, fast, viral-first. We ride trends, we create trends, we show up where students actually spend time.
- **X (Twitter):** Commentary, announcements, personality, conversations. We are quick, sharp, and never dull.

**Posting Frequency:**
- Instagram: 3 posts/week (2 Reels + 1 carousel or static)
- TikTok: 3 posts/week (all video)
- X: 3 posts/week (mix of standalone posts and engagement threads)
- Total: 9 posts/week across platforms, plus Stories/ephemeral content daily

**Hashtags to own:** #UniGram | #CampusLife | #GhanaStudents | #KNUST | #UniLife | #AfricanStudents

## WEEK 6 — "Exam Season Survival" (July 6–12)
**Narrative:** End-of-semester exams are arriving. Students are stressed. UniGram is with them. This week is empathetic, funny, and utility-driven — showing how UniGram helps during the hardest academic stretch.

**Key campus context:** Most Ghanaian universities schedule end-of-semester exams in July. Stress is real. Content should acknowledge the stress but not add to it.

**Instagram — Post 1 (July 6):** Reel — "Exam season on UniGram: how students are surviving together." Shows the study group feature, note-sharing posts, motivational content on campus feeds.
**Caption:** "Exam season hits different when your whole campus is going through it together. UniGram is your campus support system. You've got this. 📚 #UniGram #ExamSeason #GhanaStudents #CampusLife"

**Instagram — Post 2 (July 8):** Carousel — "The Exam Season Survival Guide According to KNUST Students" (real tips sourced from UniGram posts: where to study, what to eat, how to manage time, how to deal with anxiety). Show UniGram as the source of community knowledge.

**Instagram — Post 3 (July 10):** Motivational static graphic — clean, typographic, dark indigo background, white Outfit Bold text. "First class. Upper second. Third class. The degree you leave with is not the limit of what you'll become." Subtle UniGram branding at the bottom.

**TikTok:** "Things students do during exam season" relatable content | "KNUST Library at 2 AM" vibe TikTok | "Tell me you're in exam season without telling me" trend adaptation
