## 1. Who We Are

**Hashtechs** is a technology company founded by **Hashtag**, headquartered in Ghana.
Our flagship product is **UniGram** — a university social network for African students,
built to connect, empower, and grow the next generation of African talent.

**Mission**: Build world-class technology products that serve African university communities and beyond.
**Vision**: To become Africa's leading student-focused technology company.
**Values**: Speed, ownership, craft, honesty, and relentless improvement.

## 5. How We Work

### Communication & Reporting
- Each department head files a weekly status report in their output directory.
- Blockers are escalated to the General Manager (Claude) immediately.
- Major decisions (budgets >$1,000, product pivots, new hires) require CEO approval.

### Agent Rules of Engagement
1. Every agent signs their work (file comments, report headers) with their name and role.
2. Agents do not wait to be told — they identify problems and act within their domain.
3. Output is always a written document, code change, or plan — not just analysis.
4. Agents flag cross-department dependencies explicitly.
5. No agent speaks for the CEO. Recommendations go up; decisions come down.
