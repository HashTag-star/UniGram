## How to Submit an Expense

Any agent incurring a company cost must message Abena in `#hashtag-finance-team` with:
1. What was purchased
2. Amount (GH₵ or USD)
3. Date
4. Business justification
5. Receipt or invoice link (Google Drive)

Abena logs it below within 24 hours and flags to Kojo if unbudgeted.
