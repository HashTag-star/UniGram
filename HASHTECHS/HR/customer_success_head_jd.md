## About Hashtechs

Hashtechs is a technology company building UniGram — Africa's first dedicated university social network. We're a lean, fast-moving team serving Ghanaian university students, starting at KNUST and expanding across the continent. We're pre-launch, moving fast, and building something genuinely new.

---

## About the Role

We're hiring a founding Head of Customer Success to own the full post-acquisition experience for UniGram users. This is a **build-from-scratch** role — there's no team, no playbook, no existing process. You'll design and run the entire function.

You'll be responsible for ensuring students actually get value from UniGram, stay engaged, advocate for the product, and give us the feedback that makes it better. You'll also be the first point of escalation for serious user issues and the bridge between the community and the product team.

This is a **founder-mode** role. The right candidate is comfortable with zero structure, energised by building systems, and has genuine empathy for university students.

---

## What You'll Own

**User Onboarding & Activation**
- Design the onboarding journey from app install to first meaningful action
- Define activation metrics and build systems to hit them
- Work with the Tech team to surface friction points in the onboarding flow

**Retention & Engagement**
- Monitor churn signals and design interventions (in-app, push, email)
- Build a re-engagement programme for lapsed users
- Create a feedback loop from users to the product team

**Community Management**
- Be the voice of UniGram on campus — coordinate with student ambassadors
- Manage the in-app moderation escalation pipeline (working with the AI moderation layer)
- Handle sensitive user reports and appeals

**Partnerships Support**
- Work with future Partnerships team to support university-level relationships
- Coordinate with campus ambassadors on events, activations, and feedback sessions

**Metrics You'll Own**
- D7 and D30 user retention
- NPS score (target: 50+ in Year 1)
- Support ticket resolution time (target: <24h)
- Monthly Active User (MAU) contribution from CS-driven re-engagement

---

## Who You Are

**Must-haves:**
- 2–4 years of experience in customer success, community management, or product support — ideally at a consumer tech startup
- Deep familiarity with Ghanaian university culture (KNUST, UG, Ashesi, or similar)
- Excellent written communication in English; comfortable talking to students in their register
- Self-starter — you don't wait to be told, you identify problems and fix them
- Genuine passion for what UniGram is building

**Nice-to-haves:**
- Experience at a West African tech company (Paystack, Hubtel, Farmerline, etc.)
- Experience with in-app support tools (Intercom, Freshdesk, or similar)
- Active presence on the platforms our users are on (TikTok, Instagram, Twitter/X)
- Experience building ambassador programmes
